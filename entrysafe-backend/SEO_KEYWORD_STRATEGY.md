## 📊 **KEYWORD DENSITY TARGETS**

For each page, maintain natural keyword density:

| Keyword Type | Recommended Density | Example |
|--------------|---------------------|---------|
| Primary keyword | 1-2% | "accounting software South Africa" appears 3-5 times per 500 words |
| Secondary keywords | 0.5-1% | "invoice management" appears 1-3 times per 500 words |
| LSI keywords | Natural | Variations like "business accounting", "financial management" |

**Important:** Never stuff keywords. Write naturally for humans first, then optimize for search engines.
